Our mission

Cleaning, treated like real operational work.

Most cleaning businesses measure themselves by visits completed. We measure ours by surfaces actually verified, checklists signed by a real person, and the small problems prevented before they become big ones. Every recurring site at ioclean has a procedure, a primary crew that knows the space, and a paper trail.

The result is field service that quietly does its job — the site is ready, the bins are out, the bathrooms are restocked, and you can reach a Melbourne phone number on the first ring.

Our process

Four steps from first call to first clean.

01

Walk-through

20-minute site visit. We map your floors, list high-touch surfaces, and capture your operational constraints.

02

Scoped quote

Fixed-price-per-visit quote inside 24 hours. No tiered upsells, no hidden consumable charges.

03

Crew assigned

Primary cleaner + backup paired to your site. Onboarded with your specific procedures and access protocol.

04

Live reporting

Digital photo-verified checklist in your inbox after every visit. Monthly review at 90 days and beyond.

Industries
