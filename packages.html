How pricing works

Four steps from enquiry to first clean

01

Walk-through

20 minutes on-site. We measure scope, list high-touch surfaces, document access and any special requirements.

02

Fixed quote

Quoted in writing inside 24 hours. Per-visit price, breakdown of consumables, frequency and term. No surprise fees, no auto-escalation.

03

Crew assigned

A primary cleaner and a known backup are paired to your site, onboarded with your procedures and access protocol.

04

Service starts

Recurring packages begin within 5–10 business days; emergency callouts dispatch the same day, usually inside 90 minutes for metro sites.
